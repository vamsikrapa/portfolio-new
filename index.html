About Me

I started my career with a background in Computer Science, earning my Bachelor's degree before moving into the IT industry. I began at Cognizant Technology Solutions, where I worked on web development and web analytics projects, gaining a strong technical foundation. From there, I transitioned into marketing technology, focusing on tools like Tealium iQ, GTM, and Customer Data Platforms (CDP) to support data-driven campaigns.

Later, at Indegene Private Limited, I advanced into roles that combined omnichannel CRM, CDP strategy, and marketing automation, supporting life sciences and healthcare clients across global markets. My work included designing customer journeys, optimizing digital campaigns, and ensuring GDPR compliance, which allowed me to bridge the gap between technology and marketing strategy.

This gradual shift from technical development to project management and digital marketing automation inspired me to pursue my Master's in Project & Programme Management & Business Development at SKEMA Business School, Paris. Now based in Paris, I am actively seeking an internship opportunity in project management, digital marketing, or omnichannel strategy where I can apply both my technical expertise and management skills.
